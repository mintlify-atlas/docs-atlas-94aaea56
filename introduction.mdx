Energy CMMS is a full-featured ERP system built with Django, designed specifically for energy plants and industrial facilities.
</Note>

## What is Energy CMMS?

Energy CMMS is an enterprise-grade Computerized Maintenance Management System (CMMS) that provides complete control over your energy plant operations. From asset tracking to preventive maintenance, budget management to safety compliance, Energy CMMS integrates all aspects of industrial facility management into a single, powerful platform.
